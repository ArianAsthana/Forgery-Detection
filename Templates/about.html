About Document Forgery Detector

Purpose

Our tool leverages AI and image processing to detect manipulations in official documents like certificates, IDs, and contracts.

How It Works

Users upload scanned documents, which are then analyzed using AI models trained to identify pixel-level inconsistencies and tampering signs.

Visual Feedback

The system provides visual feedback by highlighting tampered regions, giving users clear insight into suspicious modifications.

Reliable Results

Backed by AI and trained datasets, our detector offers a practical solution for organizations requiring document authenticity checks.
